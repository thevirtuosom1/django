Our Experience

4S-Sphinx was founded in 1991 by Eng. Sayed Abdel Maksoud. Back then, he started it as a firm specialized in the supply and installation of closed circuit television systems. Right now, 4SSphinx is considered one of Egypt's leading companies in the field of security and surveillance systems. Our activities cover retail, commercial, and industrial solutions for projects of any size.
